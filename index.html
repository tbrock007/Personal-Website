Basketball by far my favorite sport to play and watch and for so many reasons. The way players like LeBron and Curry dominate the court is insane. I love watching the buzzer-beaters and epic dunks, and it's more than just a game. A lot of players are speaking out on important stuff, like social justice, which I think is really awesome.

Food

Plus, food's not just about eating; it's a whole vibe when you're with friends and family, sharing a meal and having a good time.
